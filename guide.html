🔐 Step 1

Setting your PIN

🔄

💡 Pick something you'll remember easily — like the last 4 digits of your phone number. You can change it anytime inside the dashboard.

📱 Step 6

Install on your iPhone

Add Clarity to your home screen so it opens like a real app — full screen, no browser bar, always one tap away.

  • Open Safari on your iPhone (must be Safari, not Chrome)
  • Go to deep21star.github.io/clarity
  • Tap the Share button — the square with an arrow pointing up, at the bottom of Safari
  • Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  • Name it "Clarity" → tap Add
  • Find the icon on your home screen and tap it — opens full-screen like a real app ✓
📱 Once installed, check it on pay day. Mark your bills, check your balance. Takes 60 seconds and keeps you in control of your money.
☁ Step 7

Sync across all your devices

Want your data on both your laptop and your iPhone? Set up Cloud Sync — it's free and takes 2 minutes.

✓ Once set up, every change you make saves to the cloud automatically. Any device with your Clarity link will load the same data.
  • Go to jsonbin.io and create a free account (no credit card needed)
  • Click "Create Bin" — name it "Clarity" — paste in {} as the content and save
  • Copy the Bin ID shown at the top (looks like: 6618a3...)
  • Go to Account → API Keys — copy your Master Key
  • Open Clarity, tap the ☁ SETUP SYNC button at the top of your dashboard
  • Paste your Bin ID and Master Key → tap Save & Sync — done ✓
  • Repeat the last step on your other devices — they'll all share the same data from now on
🔒 Your data is stored privately in your own JSONBin account. Whetū Digital cannot see your financial data.
❓ FAQ

Common questions

I forgot my PIN — what do I do?

Message Deep at Whetū Digital and he'll reset it for you. A self-serve reset is coming in a future update.

My laptop data doesn't show on my iPhone — is that normal?

Set up Cloud Sync (see the ☁ Sync section above) — it's free and takes 2 minutes. After that, all your devices share the same data automatically. The ☁ SETUP SYNC button is at the top of your dashboard.

Is my data private? Who can see it?

Your data is stored only in your browser on your device. No one else can see it. The PIN stops anyone who picks up your phone from getting in. Your actual bank account details are never stored in Clarity.

My electricity bill changed — how do I update it?

Click the amount in the Utilities section, type the new number, press Enter. It saves instantly. Update it whenever you get a new bill.

Can I use this on my Android phone?

Yes. Open Chrome on Android, go to the Clarity URL, and tap the three-dot menu → "Add to Home Screen". Works just like the iPhone.

How do I change the currency?

There's a currency selector at the top of your dashboard. Tap it to switch between AUD, NZD, USD, GBP or EUR.
