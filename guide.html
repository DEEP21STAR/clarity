Here's how Clarity works

Clarity is your personal finance dashboard. It keeps all your bills in one place, shows what you've spent, and helps you see where your money is going — in plain English, no spreadsheets needed.

📊
See everything at once
Your income, expenses, and balance — always up to date, on any device.
✓
Mark bills as paid
One tap when a bill is paid. Clarity tracks what's done so you don't have to.
📱
Works on your phone
Open in Safari or Chrome. Add it to your home screen like a real app.
🔒
Private & secure
Protected by a 4-digit PIN. Your data stays on your device.

7
Syncing across your devices
Clarity can save your data to the cloud so it's the same on your phone and laptop. Free to set up.
☁️What sync does: Without sync, your data only lives on one device. With sync on, any change on your phone will appear on your laptop too — and vice versa.
1
Tap SYNC at the top right of the dashboard.
2
Deep has set up your sync account. Ask him for your Bin ID and API Key if you need to enter them.
3
Once entered, the top right shows ✓ SYNCED. Your data saves automatically from then on.
📱iPhone home screen tip: Open Clarity in Safari → tap the Share button (box with arrow) → tap "Add to Home Screen". It opens like a real app with no browser bar.
8
Changing your PIN
You can change your PIN any time from within the dashboard.
1
On the dashboard, tap HELP (top right).
2
Find and tap Change PIN.
3
Enter your current PIN, then choose and confirm a new 4-digit PIN.
⚠️Write your PIN down somewhere safe when you first create it. If you forget it, message Deep at hello@whetudigital.co.nz and he can reset it for you.
